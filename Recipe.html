Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: 45 mins
Serving : 8

Steps Below : Click start Cooking

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 2 cups of sugar
  • 1.5 cups of choco powder
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 2 eggs or if you want eggless Dont add eggs

Steps

  1. Slightly Heat the oven with 350°F ( that means 175°C).
  2. Mix all the ingredients.
  3. Add the wet ingredients and mix until it becomes smooth.
  4. Apply the butter into a greased pan.
  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
